About Us

We are Anima Corvus, a metal band from Granada (Spain). Our musical genre may be defined as Melodic Metalcore.


The band was born in March 2017. With only two months, they play at the festival "Katillo Rock", this is a local festival but that year there were bands like SOMAS CURE, KTULU CARDIAC and GODDAMN.


In the month of August 2017, they started recording their first EP.


The components of the band have a lot of experience on the stage and they demostrate it in every performance.
